"God does not place dice with the markets."

There are reasons for and processes that govern everything that happen. Nothing in this physical world is truly random, including markets. However, many events and processes that occur are not directly observable. Randomness is an abstraction used to compensate the limited perception of and lack of understanding about the world. The question then becomes, how can we best approximate certain events given limited information?

Qian Capital takes both an epistemological and scientific approach to answer these questions and gain a competitive edge in the markets:
Epistemological reasoning and understanding are essential for great investment ideas, and understanding the world more deeply.
The Scientific Process and quantitative analysis validate or reshape our ideas.
AI algorithms, with much greater pattern recognition abilities and reaction time than humans, are taught these ideas and deployed to the markets.
